VIRTUOSITY

A symposium about Art, Entertainment and IT.

Every year, study association Inter-Actief organizes a symposium. This year, the theme will be the wonderful world of Art, Entertainment and their connections with IT. This encapsulates subjects such as interactive environments, auditive and visual technologies and simulation and animation of imagery. The symposium will take place in or around Enschede on the 15th of March, 2017.

Symposium

Symposium Virtuosity will be filled with 7 different lectures from 7 different lecturers. Inbetween the lectures, there will be a lunch and coffee/tea breaks. To end the day properly, there wil be a closing reception.

Art, Entertainment and IT

Symposium Virtuosity has three main subjects that are going to be addressed during the symposium, these are Interactive Environments, Auditory- and Visual- Technologies and Data Processing, Simulation and Manipulation of Pictures and Animation.

Date

Symposium Virtuosity will take place on the 15th of March in 2017. The symposium will start at 9:30 AM and end around 6:00 PM.

Location

Symposium Virtuosity will take place at Concordia. Concordia is a theater established in Enschede's city center.
